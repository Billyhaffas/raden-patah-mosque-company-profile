Our Story

In 2004, two MIT graduate students set out to reshape how companies grow. Instead of interrupting buyers with ads, the idea was to be helpful—create content people actually want. Over time, the platform evolved to power marketing, sales, and service for organizations of all sizes.

Along the way the company expanded globally while staying focused on a crafted, connected product suite that helps teams scale customer relationships with empathy and data.
